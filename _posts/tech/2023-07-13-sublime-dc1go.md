> Sublime在使用中“径”、“隔”、“增”三个字显示不正常可以调整Sublime设置修改

Preferences --> settings中右侧添加代码

```
"font_size": 13,
"dpi_scale": 1.0,
"font_face": "YaHei Consolas hybrid",
"ignored_packages":
[
    "Vintage"
],
"font_options":
[
    "gdi"
],
```
